# Idea portfolio ladder — after GenAI coding foundations

**When:** after learners understand **brief → implement → verify** and org **data/tool** guardrails (core modules + capstone practice).

**Purpose:** channel energy into a **portfolio of ideas** sized **small / medium / large**—and into an explicit **effort & token budget** so they don’t boil the ocean or starve the marquee bet.

This is **strategy coaching inside the platform**, not generic brainstorming.

---

## Ladder structure (required shape)

| Bucket | Count | Intent | Typical horizon* |
|--------|-------|--------|------------------|
| **Small** | **1** first | Prove the loop on a **tiny** real or synthetic slice; confidence | hours–1 day |
| **Medium** | **several** (2–5) | Compound habits; vary data sensitivity & surfaces | days–2 weeks each |
| **Large** | **1** | North-star outcome; needs sponsor air cover & review bandwidth | weeks–quarter |

\*Facilitator calibrates—regulated environments may stretch “small” purely synthetic.

**Sequencing discipline:** learner **ships or demonstrates small #1** before locking large scope. Medium ideas can **parallel** only if **independent** (no shared blocker).

---

## What “tokens / effort” means (disambiguate for UX)

In product copy, offer **three sliders** (orgs may hide some):

1. **Focus time** — hours/week the learner (and sponsor) commit.  
2. **LLM / tooling budget** — if the employer caps API $ or Copilot seats by team (optional field).  
3. **Human review cycles** — buddy/mentor slots (scarce resource = real constraint).

Avoid implying learners must optimize **API tokens** technically unless that’s an explicit enterprise module.

---

## Guided decision flow (in-app / worksheet)

1. **Brain dump** — unordered list (10–20 bullets), no judgment.  
2. **Classify** — S / M / L using rubric below.  
3. **Kill / park** — ruthless; cap **M** at max N (e.g. 5) for the cohort window.  
4. **Dependency graph** — arrows; **L** must not depend on unparked fantasies.  
5. **Budget fit** — sum of S+M+L **rough** hours vs weekly budget; trim.  
6. **Commit** — one **S** with acceptance tests **this week**; schedule first **M**; **L** gets “decision review” date with buddy.  
7. **Rebalance gate** — week 2 retrospective: demote **L**→**M** if evidence says no.

Artifact: [`templates/idea-portfolio-template.md`](../templates/idea-portfolio-template.md).

---

## Sizing rubric (starter)

| Signal | Small | Medium | Large |
|--------|-------|--------|-------|
| Acceptance tests | 1–3 crisp | several + edge cases | suite + stakeholders |
| Data | synthetic or public | internal sandbox | prod-adjacent / multi-system |
| Blast radius if wrong | negligible | team annoyance | org / client / safety |
| Reviews needed | self + optional buddy | buddy + maybe lead | formal sign-off path |

---

## Facilitator / intelligence hooks

- [**`learner-profile-template.md`**](../templates/learner-profile-template.md) feeds **domain** vocabulary for idea titles.  
- **Jurisdiction**: large ideas auto-surface **red-flag** if data class = sensitive (link compliance pack).  
- **Gamification**: completing **S** unlocks “Portfolio unlock” badge; **L** checklist green = capstone-tier reward.

---

## Non-goals

- Choosing **L** for learner in top-down way without their sentence of commitment  
- Portfolio as infinite backlog—**force** cap on **M** during fixed cohort
