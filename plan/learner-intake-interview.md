# Per-learner intake interview (after enrollment, before Week 1 live)

**Audience:** each **enrolled** SME, 1:1 with facilitator (or TA). **Not** the same as market [`interview-script.md`](interview-script.md)—that one screens demand; this one **designs the capstone around the human**.

**Elite respondents** (partner, GC, EVP, distinguished engineer, founder): run **async prep first** via [`conversation-intelligence-prep.md`](conversation-intelligence-prep.md)—then shorten the live intake.

**Budget:** **35–45 minutes** (calendar 50); elite track often **25–35 min** live if prep brief already exists.  
**Goal:** map **where their domain authority lives**, **industry + jurisdiction**, and **what fires them up**—so practice work uses *their* vocabulary and motivation, and compliance hooks to the right [`jurisdiction-profile-template.md`](../templates/jurisdiction-profile-template.md).

**Output:** completed [`templates/learner-profile-template.md`](../templates/learner-profile-template.md) saved as e.g. `plan/learners/<anonymous-slug>.md` (see [`plan/learners/README.md`](learners/README.md) for PII guardrails).

---

## Consent & logistics (3 min)

- Explain notes inform facilitation and capstone tailoring; capstone brief still goes through normal templates.
- Where may this document live (internal wiki vs this repo)? Any **names/employers** redacted in shared copies?
- Confirm preferred name / pronouns; timezone; accessibility needs.

## Placement: industry & country (8 min) — **critical**

These fields drive **regulator set**, **data rules**, and realistic example tasks.

| Capture | Ask naturally |
|---------|----------------|
| **Employer industry** | “What sector is your org in one sentence—banking, hospital system, manufacturer…?” |
| **Your function** | Team name + who consumes your work. |
| **Primary country of employment** | Where is your contract and day-to-day workplace? |
| **State / province / region** | Needed for many privacy and employment overlays. |
| **Other countries** you touch (customers, data residency, remote colleagues) | |

If answers differ from cohort-level [`charter-v0.md`](charter-v0.md) assumptions, **flag** for legal pack or sponsor.

## Domain authority map (12 min) — **where is your knowledge?**

- “If you had to **teach a new hire for a week**, what are the **three buckets** you’d own end-to-end?”
- “What do colleagues **ping you** for that isn’t in your job description?”
- “Where are you **fluent** vs **still learning**?” (no shame—maps support needs)
- “What **artifact** best proves your expertise—a spec, a model, a spreadsheet, a narrative, a checklist?”
- “What **jargon** must the AI never misuse in your world?” (terms to protect in briefs)

Summarize in their profile: **core domain**, **boundary** (edges of expertise), **credibility artifacts**.

## Passion & energy (10 min) — **what they care about most**

Passion predicts **persistence** through friction; use it to pick capstone *framing*, not fluff.

- “When time disappears at work—**what problem were you chasing**?”
- “**Proudest** outcome in the last 12–24 months—what role did judgment play?”
- “If this AI+coding cohort went **spectacularly well**, **who benefits** besides you—and what changed for them?”
- “Caused you to **grind anyway** lately even when bureaucracy fought you?” 
- Non-work: hobbies or communities—**anything you’d enjoy** bridging into a synthetic exercise?

**Trap to avoid:** do not coerce “passion”; some people anchor on **duty** or **care**—“what you refuse to half-ass?” works as alternate.

## Tooling & data reality (7 min)

- Blessed tools today; blocker tools.
- **Three concrete tasks** worth automating (even if imperfect)—prioritize alignment with domain map above.
- **Hard red lines**: data/systems AI must never touch in learning.

## AI coding headspace (5 min)

- Shortcut to [`interview-script.md`](interview-script.md) latent-need prompts if not already captured.
- Confidence 1–5 on reading diffs, running scripts, naming acceptance criteria—**after** reassurance there’s no grade.

## Close & next step (3 min)

- Agree **one provisional capstone direction** (“direction” not locked scope)—one sentence each from facilitator + learner.
- Deadline to return **filled problem-brief draft** before session 01 (or starter bullets if brief feels heavy).
- Point to [`learner-profile-template.md`](../templates/learner-profile-template.md) fill during or right after call.

---

## Facilitator checklist (immediately after call)

- [ ] `learner-profile` file created with **industry, country, sub-jurisdiction, passion line, domain map**.
- [ ] Mismatch vs cohort `jurisdiction-profile` / `legal-packs` noted for sponsor.
- [ ] If regulated data: **synthetic path** confirmed for their capstone direction.
- [ ] Buddy or peer pairing hint recorded (personas doc).
